Here is your ultimate guide to gifting!

Christmas time is all about giving! Here is a step by step guide on how to gift!

Step one:

Get started early! You never know if you are going to have to order soemthing from a distant country. Also the best gifts take time!

Step two:

Get to know what type of stuff they tend to like. Also if they have mentioned wanting anything. They might even have a gift list!

Step three:

Keep it a surpise! This makes it fun and exciting!

Step four:

This is when you decide on the main structure of the gift! You can choose how many gifts, and if there is a theme. Maybe you are making a cool layout, with multiple layers of paper, or in a cute basket!

Step five:

Now you can buy, order, or make their gift.

Step six:

Now wrap it up in the neatest way possible, put in a cute bag, or in a nice box! Add a name tag or card.

Step four:

Make sure it is from the heart, and place it beneath the Christmas tree!
