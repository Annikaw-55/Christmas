Hello there, welcome to your guide to having an amazing Christmas, even though it won't be spent with others

First off I would like to say, this could be a lot more fun with others. Please, for the sake of your enjoyment, try your best to make friends or invite family over. You can still have a blast without anybody else though. Here is a list of a few ideas that would be so much fun by yourself!

  1. Watch Christmas shows
  2. Bake cookies
  3. Play in the snow
  4. Sing Christmas songs
  5. Go to Christmas shows and concerts

Here are a few more ideas! You have got this thing! Never lose Christmas spirit!
